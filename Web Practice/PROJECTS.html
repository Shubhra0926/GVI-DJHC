Reservoir Community Farm

Reservoir Community Farm is a 1.7 acre educational farm right in the heart of Bridgeport with over 47 plots for community gardeners to grow food for their families.

! At the farm, GVI and our community members have grown and sold thousands of pounds of affordable produce each year for the community.

Reservoir Community Farm also served as a setting for educational school field trips, the site for our Youth Leadership Program, and a knowledge resource hub for Bridgeport’s school and community garden network.

Community Gardens

Green Village Initiative previously supported 12 Community Gardens that nourish the Bridgeport community.

As our organization is in a period of transition, we are transferring management of the garden sites directly to the gardeners and offering them support to protect these places for community and urban growing.

Youth Leadership Program with Green Village Initiative

Green Village Initiative’s Youth Leadership Program strived to cultivate young food leaders in Bridgeport by developing skills in agricultural knowledge, food justice, career readiness, and leadership.
